Our Story

HealThML was founded in 1986 by Hentry Teal and Micheal Leiung, with the goal of providing top-quality private healthcare nationwide.

In the following 40 years, HealThML has served over 300,000 satisfied customers, with the help of our talented team of 1000 staff worldwide.
