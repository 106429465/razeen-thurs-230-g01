Clinical Care Coordinator HMLC1

Role description

A frontline role responsible for guiding patients through their care journey, ensuring clear communication, timely support, and seamless coordination between clinicians, families, and service teams.

Salary & Reporting Line

  • Salary: AUD $68,000-$78,000 per year (depending on experience)
  • Reports to: Senior Clinical Operations Manager

Key Responsibilities

  • Coordinate patient appointments, follow-ups, and care pathways.
  • Maintain accurate records and ensure privacy compliance.
  • Communicate clearly with clinicians, families, and support teams.
  • Monitor caseload progress and escalate issues when needed.

Essential Requirements

  1. Experience in healthcare admin, patient coordination, or support services.
  2. Strong communication skills and empathy in patient interactions.
  3. Confident using digital systems and managing detailed records.
  4. Ability to work collaboratively in a multidisciplinary team.

Preferable Requirements

  • Certificate or Diploma in Health Administration or related field.
  • Experience in private healthcare or allied health settings.
  • Familiarity with clinical terminology and workflows.
Health Services Support Officer HMLA7

Role description

A service delivery role focused on operational support, patient assistance, and ensuring smooth day-to-day functioning of HealThML's care environment.

Salary & Reporting Line

  • Salary: AUD $58,000-$66,000 per year
  • Reports to: Operations & Service Delivery Lead

Key Responsibilities

  • Support daily operations including scheduling and documentation.
  • Assist clinicians by preparing resources and managing workflow tasks.
  • Provide friendly, accessible assistance to patients and visitors.
  • Maintain organised administrative systems aligned with HealThML standards.

Essential Requirements

  1. Experience in admin, customer service, or support roles.
  2. Strong organisational and multitasking skills.
  3. Confident using digital tools and communication platforms.
  4. Demonstrated reliability, initiative, and professionalism.

Preferable Requirements

  • Experience in healthcare, disability, or community services.
  • Understanding of privacy and confidentiality requirements.
  • Training in first aid or similar support-focused certifications.
